Pretérito Indefinido (Simple Past)

Master the Spanish simple past tense

Understanding the Simple Past

The pretérito indefinido is used to describe completed actions in the past. It's equivalent to the English simple past tense (e.g., "I walked", "She sang").

Quick Tip

Think of the pretérito indefinido as the "snapshot" tense - it captures specific, completed moments in the past.

Ayer caminé al parque.

Yesterday I walked to the park.

Regular Verbs

Subject -ar (hablar) -er (comer) -ir (vivir)
yo hablé comí viví
tú hablaste comiste viviste
él/ella/usted habló comió vivió
nosotros hablamos comimos vivimos
vosotros hablasteis comisteis vivisteis
ellos/ellas/ustedes hablaron comieron vivieron

Important Note

Notice that -er and -ir verbs share the same endings in the pretérito indefinido!

Common Irregular Verbs

Some of the most frequently used Spanish verbs are irregular in the pretérito indefinido. These need to be memorized as they are among the most common words in the language.

Subject ser/ir estar hacer
yo fui estuve hice
tú fuiste estuviste hiciste
él/ella/usted fue estuvo hizo

Compound Irregular Verb Patterns

Compund verbs are verbs which contain another verb. When the contained verb is irregular in the simple past tense (pretérito indefinido) the compund follows the same pattern of irregulartiy. Learning these patterns can help you master multiple verbs at once!

Pattern Recognition

Verbs that are related (like 'tener' and 'contener') usually share the same irregularity in the past tense.

Pattern Example Verbs Yo Form Él/Ella Form
-tener verbs tener, contener, mantener, obtener tuve, contuve tuvo, contuvo
-ducir verbs producir, conducir, traducir produje, conduje produjo, condujo
-traer verbs traer, atraer, contraer traje, atraje trajo, atrajo
-poner verbs poner, componer, disponer puse, compuse puso, compuso

Stem Changing Irregular Forms

Some common verbs have a change in theri stem when conjugated in the past tense:

Stem Changes

Many irregular verbs change their stems in all forms. For example:

  • dormir → durm- (durmió, durmieron)
  • pedir → pid- (pidió, pidieron)
  • servir → sirv- (sirvió, sirvieron)
Stem Change Infinitive Yo Tú Él/Ella Ellos/Ellas
o->u poder (can) pude pudiste pudo pudieron
morir (die) morí moriste murió murieron
dormir (sleep) dormí dormiste durmió dormieron
e->i ver (to see) vi viste vio vieron
decir (to say) dije dijiste dijo dijeron
venir (to come) vine viniste vino vinieron

Examples:

El profesor no me mantuvo después de clase. (The teacher didn't keep me after class.)

Los estudiantes tradujeron el texto. (The students translated the text.)

¿Quién puso las llaves aquí? (Who put the keys here?)

Two Ways to Talk About the Past in Spanish

Spanish has two different past tenses for different types of past actions:

Simple Past (Pretérito Indefinido)

For completed actions with clear start/end points.

Think: "I ate", "She went", "They arrived"

Past Imperfect (Pretérito Imperfecto)

For ongoing or repeated actions in the past.

Think: "I used to eat", "She was going", "It was raining"

English Analogy

Imagine describing your childhood home:

  • Simple Past: "We moved into that house in 1995." (One-time, completed action)
    Nos mudamos a esa casa en el año mil novecientos noventa y cinco.
  • Past Imperfect: "Our house was blue and had a big garden." (Ongoing state/description)
    Nuestra casa era azul y tenía un jardín grande.

Or think about a party:

  • Simple Past: "I arrived at 8pm." (Specific moment)
    Llegué a las ocho de la noche.
  • Past Imperfect: "People were dancing when I arrived." (Ongoing action punctuated by specific moment)
    La gente bailaba cuando llegué.

Time Expressions

These time expressions are commonly used with the pretérito indefinido:

  • ayer (yesterday)
  • la semana pasada (last week)
  • el mes pasado (last month)
  • el año pasado (last year)
  • hace dos días (two days ago)

Cultural Practice Exercises

Practice past tense while learning about Spanish and Latin American culture

Historical Figures and Events

Diego Rivera was one of Mexico's most famous artists, known for his murals depicting Mexican society and history.

Diego Rivera ________ (pintar) muchos murales en la Ciudad de México.

This is a completed action in the past. Use the él/ella form of pintar in pretérito indefinido.

Rivera's murals can still be seen today in the National Palace and other buildings.

Traditions and Celebrations

Las Fallas is a traditional celebration in Valencia, Spain, known for its elaborate artistic monuments that are burned at the end of the festival.

El año pasado, miles de personas ________ (asistir) a las Fallas de Valencia.

Use the ellos/ellas form of asistir in pretérito indefinido.

Las Fallas was declared Intangible Cultural Heritage by UNESCO in 2016.

Food and Cuisine

Paella is a traditional Spanish dish that originated in Valencia. It's made with rice, saffron, and various combinations of meat, seafood, and vegetables.

Mi abuela ________ (aprender) a cocinar paella cuando ________ (vivir) en Valencia.

First blank: specific moment of learning (indefinido). Second blank: lived there for a period (imperfecto).

Traditional Valencian paella is made with rabbit, chicken, and local vegetables.

Literature and Arts

Gabriel García Márquez was a Colombian writer who won the Nobel Prize in Literature. He is famous for his magical realism style.

García Márquez ________ (escribir) "Cien años de soledad" mientras ________ (vivir) en México.

Writing the book was completed (indefinido), living in Mexico was ongoing (imperfecto).

"Cien años de soledad" (One Hundred Years of Solitude) was published in 1967 and is considered one of the most significant works in Spanish literature.

Sports

The Spanish national football team, "La Roja," has been one of the most successful teams in football history. ⚽

España ________ (ganar) el Mundial mientras yo ________ (estudiar) en Madrid.

Winning is a specific event (indefinido), studying was ongoing (imperfecto).

Spain won their first FIFA World Cup in 2010, defeating the Netherlands in the final.
